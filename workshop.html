Welcome to Wave

Wave Surf School has been dedicated to providing good quality and value for money surf tours since 1999. We have developed our teaching methods and we are based around the best-uncrowded waves to guarantee you will be standing and riding a wave in no time! Make use of our surf camp and remote surfing locations. Join the crew from Waves Surf School so you can enjoy the thrill of riding waves and the laid back Aussie surfing lifestyle.
