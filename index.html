Surf camp

Wave Surf Camp – Seal Rocks

Seal Rocks is renowned for its great waves and natural beauty. Located only 4 hours North of Sydney on the warm North Coast. Seal Rocks has long been considered one of the last frontiers of the North Coast. The surf camp is nestled between beautiful National Parks where the lakes meet the ocean. With many Kilometers of pristine uncrowded beach accessible by bus and 4WD. Enjoy the range of the facilities at the surf camp and the best waves

Surf Camp highlights:

  • Swimming pool & hot tub
  • Camp Fire
  • Surf Camp Beach Parties

Lessons

Wave Surf school offers group packages! Group lessons begin with four people and are no larger than eight people. Meet new friends who like you are learning to surf. You may also arrange your own groups of 4 people minimum. Group lessons are only €35,- per person.

What is expected in a lesson

  • 4-8 peopleper session (2hr) per intructor
  • Beach instruction
  • Lessons in the water
  • Lessons begin at 10 am, 2:00 pm, 4:00pm, 7 days a week.

What do you need to bring?
